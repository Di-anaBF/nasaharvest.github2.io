NASA Harvest is NASA's agriculture and food security program, implemented as a multi-sectoral Consortium led by the University of Maryland. Our mission is to enable and advance awareness, use, and adoption of satellite Earth observations by public and private organizations to benefit food security and agriculture in the US and worldwide. You can read more about NASA Harvest on our website.
