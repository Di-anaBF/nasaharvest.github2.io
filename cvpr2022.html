Machine Learning for Remote Sensing: Agriculture and Food Security

This tutorial will cover fundamental topics of machine learning for remote sensing applications in agriculture and food security, focusing on the African context. You can find the full list of tutorials on the CVPR 2022 website. You must be registered for the conference to attend the tutorial, which is being held in person.
