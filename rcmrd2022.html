Scalable Cropland Mapping Workshop

Monday August 22, 2022 - Thursday August 25, 2022
In person: Room 301 at 4600 River Road, Riverdale Park, MD
Virtual: join through https://umd.zoom.us/my/izvonkov
Public Google Doc: link
Public Google Drive: link

This workshop will do a deep dive into scalable cropland mapping with deep learning. Fundamental topics in machine learning, satellite time series data, labeling, and scalable systems will be covered. Every participant will generate their own project for cropland mapping with deep learning.
