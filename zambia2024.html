Zambia Cropland Mapping Training 2024

Dates: June 11-14, 2024

The University of Maryland NASA Harvest Program, through its partners, supports governments in the application of Earth Observation for crop conditions monitoring through expansion of the GEOGLAM Crop Monitors. These efforts seek to address the critical gap in available rapid assessment data and reporting on crop conditions for multi-level food security decision making, and to complement efforts by ministries of agriculture and other institutions collecting relevant data. The implementation of the Zambia National Crop Monitor will enable the development of an actionable information tool and a soft copy synthesis report that draws from existing sources and data collection systems.
