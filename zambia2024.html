Zambia Crop Monitoring Training 2024

Schedule  |  Platforms  |  Whiteboard  |  Post-Training Survey

Dates: June 11-14, 2024

The University of Maryland NASA Harvest Program, through its partners, supports governments in the application of Earth Observation for crop conditions monitoring through expansion of the GEOGLAM Crop Monitors. These efforts seek to address the critical gap in available rapid assessment data and reporting on crop conditions for multi-level food security decision making, and to complement efforts by ministries of agriculture and other institutions collecting relevant data. This training, therefore, seeks to transfer the required skill set to generate more reliable, actionable information to support decision-making using the National Crop Monitor and as well bring together key stakeholders from Zambia, Kenya, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Rwanda and Uganda.
